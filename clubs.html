How to Join a Club

  1. Browse and Research: Review the clubs listed above and visit their social media pages or notice boards to learn more about their activities and upcoming events.
  2. Attend a Meeting: Most clubs welcome visitors to their regular meetings. Check the meeting times and locations posted on campus notice boards or ask current members.
  3. Contact Club Leadership: Reach out to the club president or secretary via email or approach them during campus events. They can provide you with membership requirements and upcoming activities.
  4. Fill Out Membership Form: Complete the club's membership application form, which typically includes your contact information, year of study, and reasons for joining.
  5. Pay Membership Fees: Most clubs require a small membership fee (usually KES 500-1500 per semester) to cover club activities and resources.
  6. Attend Orientation: New members often attend an orientation session to learn about club rules, upcoming projects, and how to get involved in leadership roles.
  7. Start Participating: Actively engage in club meetings, events, and projects. The more you participate, the more you'll benefit from the experience!
